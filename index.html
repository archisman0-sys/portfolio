01
RAG Knowledge Engine · Edvoy Production
Genie — AI Knowledge Engine
Problem Statement
Students exploring study-abroad options had to navigate hundreds of university pages, visa policy docs, and scholarship portals to get answers. Counsellor queues were overloaded and response times were too slow for a global, always-on student base across 90+ countries.
A production RAG system that powers intelligent Q&A across Edvoy's entire knowledge base — universities, courses, visa, scholarships, FAQs — serving 1M+ students with sub-second responses and hallucination guardrails.
🎯
Planning & Strategy▶
  • Defined RAG architecture vision for multi-domain queries across 90+ countries
  • Mapped knowledge domains: 750+ institutions, visa policies, scholarships, FAQs
  • Prioritised semantic vs fixed-size chunking strategy per content type
  • Aligned on latency SLAs, accuracy thresholds, and hallucination guardrails
🎨
Product Design & UX▶
  • Conversational Q&A interface with source attribution and confidence signals
  • Fallback flows for low-confidence retrievals with escalation to counsellors
  • Thumbs up/down feedback loop to continuously retrain retriever quality
  • Admin panel for knowledge ingestion, refresh scheduling, and monitoring
📝
Content & Knowledge Base▶
  • Audited and structured 750+ institutional data sources with metadata schema
  • Built content refresh pipelines for live data sync with institutional feeds
  • Wrote system prompts, Genie persona, and tone guidelines
  • Created QA test sets per domain to benchmark retrieval accuracy
⚙️
Technical Build & Environments▶
  • Vector DB (Pinecone/Weaviate), embedding pipeline, chunking, re-ranking layer
  • LLM integration with context injection and guardrails against hallucination
  • Docker containerisation, CI/CD, staging → production environment promotion
  • Latency optimisation: caching layer, async retrieval, batch embedding jobs
🚀
Deployment & GTM▶
  • Phased rollout: internal QA → counsellor beta → student-facing production
  • KPIs defined: query resolution rate, CSAT, support ticket deflection %
  • In-app onboarding with seeded example questions to drive adoption
  • Post-launch conversation quality audits and weekly prompt iteration
↓ Support ticket volumeSub-second Q&A at 1M+ scale90+ countries coveredAlways-on student support

Comprehensive HR SaaS with three product modules: Employee Self-Service, Organisation Management, and HR Operations. AI layer powers performance analysis, attrition risk scoring, org health metrics, and an HR policy Q&A chatbot — built as a full multi-tenant product.

🎨
Product Design — 3 Surfaces▶
